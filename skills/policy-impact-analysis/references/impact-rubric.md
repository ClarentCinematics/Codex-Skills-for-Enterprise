# Policy Impact Rubric

## Impact Dimensions

| Dimension | Questions |
| --- | --- |
| Legal/compliance | What obligation, deadline, or evidence is required? |
| Operational | Which workflows, teams, or approvals change? |
| Technical | Which systems, data flows, controls, or logs are affected? |
| Customer/vendor | Which external commitments or third parties are affected? |
| Governance | Who must approve, monitor, or attest? |

## Requirement Classification

- **Must**: explicit requirement or prohibition.
- **Should**: strong recommendation or control expectation.
- **May**: permission or optional path.
- **Interpretation**: operational reading that requires human review.

## Action Plan Pattern

| Priority | Requirement | Gap | Action | Owner | Due date | Review needed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Critical | ... | ... | ... | Not stated | Not stated | Legal / Compliance / Security |

## Human Review Triggers

- legal interpretation;
- regulated data or customer commitments;
- security/privacy control changes;
- contractual, procurement, or financial exposure.
