# CRM Hygiene Rubric

## Common Hygiene Issues

| Issue | Signal | Business impact |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Missing owner | blank owner, unclear handoff | accountability gap |
| Stale next step | old activity date, no future action | deal momentum risk |
| Weak stage evidence | stage advanced without buyer signal | forecast inflation |
| Missing amount/date | blank value, close date absent | pipeline reporting risk |
| Duplicate account/contact | similar names/domains | fragmented history |
| Unclear decision process | no buyer, champion, criteria | qualification risk |
| Unsupported forecast | confidence claim without evidence | leadership trust risk |

## Severity

- **Critical**: affects revenue forecast, customer commitment, compliance, or ownership.
- **High**: blocks sales motion or manager inspection.
- **Medium**: reduces quality but does not block action.
- **Low**: cleanup or formatting issue.
