# Deflection Rubric

Use this rubric to classify repeated support demand.

## Deflection Types

- KB article: repeated how-to, setup, troubleshooting, or policy question.
- Macro: repeated support response that still needs agent judgment.
- Routing change: repeated misrouted tickets or unclear intake categories.
- Product feedback: repeated friction caused by confusing UI, missing affordance, or unreliable behavior.
- Automation candidate: deterministic request with clear inputs, safe actions, and reviewable output.

## Review Risks

- Ticket subjects alone rarely prove root cause.
- Frequency in a small sample does not equal full support volume.
- Similar wording can reflect different customer situations.
- Do not publish customer-specific workarounds as general guidance without review.
