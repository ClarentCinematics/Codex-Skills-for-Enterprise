# Account Research Patterns

## Evidence Labels

- **Sourced fact**: directly supported by provided material.
- **Reasonable inference**: plausible from facts, but not directly stated.
- **Hypothesis**: useful idea that requires validation.
- **Unknown**: important missing information.

## ICP Fit Areas

| Area | Signals |
| --- | --- |
| Firmographic | size, industry, geography, maturity |
| Operational | team structure, workflows, complexity |
| Technical | tools, integrations, data readiness |
| Commercial | budget signal, urgency, buying cycle |
| Strategic | initiative alignment, executive priority |

## Outreach Angle Pattern

```text
Angle: <specific business issue>
Evidence: <sourced fact or labeled inference>
Message hook: <short, relevant opener>
Discovery question: <question that validates the angle>
```
