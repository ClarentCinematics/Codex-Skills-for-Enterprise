# Vendor Security Rubric

Use this rubric to prepare reviewable vendor-security packets.

## Core Topics

- SOC2 or equivalent independent assurance.
- Data processing agreement and privacy terms.
- Subprocessor disclosure and change notification.
- Data retention, deletion, and export.
- Breach notification timing and contact path.
- Encryption in transit and at rest.
- SSO, MFA, RBAC, and account lifecycle controls.
- Audit logs and administrative visibility.

## Review Risks

- Vendor claims without evidence.
- Security controls described as planned rather than available.
- Missing data retention or deletion terms.
- No breach-notification commitment.
- Subprocessors not disclosed.
- Ambiguous handling of regulated or sensitive data.

## Safe Language

- Use "coverage gap" for absent answers.
- Use "requires security/legal review" for approvals, exceptions, and contractual interpretation.
- Do not certify compliance or accept risk.
