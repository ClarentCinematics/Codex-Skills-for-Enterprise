# Evidence Rubric

## Confidence Levels

| Level | Criteria |
| --- | --- |
| High | multiple reliable sources, recent evidence, consistent findings |
| Medium | credible evidence but limited scope, age, or agreement |
| Low | weak, indirect, old, anecdotal, or conflicting evidence |
| Unknown | insufficient evidence to assess |

## Source Quality Signals

- publication date and relevance;
- primary vs secondary source;
- methodology and sample size;
- commercial or advocacy bias;
- consistency with other sources;
- directness to the research question.

## Contradiction Handling

- State the disagreement directly.
- Explain what each source supports.
- Identify likely reasons for the difference.
- Recommend what would resolve the uncertainty.
